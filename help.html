Welcome to FlyEase Help!

If you're facing issues with our flight booking services, you've come to the right place. Below are some common questions and answers that might help you.

Common Questions

  • How do I book a flight on FlyEase?
    To book a flight on FlyEase, simply visit our website, enter your travel details, including destination, travel dates, and number of passengers, and choose from available flight options.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept credit cards, debit cards, and online banking as payment methods. You can find more details about payment options during the booking process.
  • Can I change or cancel my flight booking?
    Yes, you can change or cancel your flight booking based on the fare rules of your ticket. Please visit the 'Manage Booking' section on our website for detailed instructions.
  • How do I check my flight status?
    You can check your flight status by entering your flight details on the 'Flight Status' page of our website or by contacting our customer support team.
  • Do you offer travel insurance?
    Yes, we offer travel insurance options during the booking process to provide coverage for unexpected events during your trip.
